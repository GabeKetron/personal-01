Job Posting Analysis : Biomedical Vs Software Engineer from Cybercoder

   Nowadays, the data science field is hot, and it is unlikely that this will change in the near future. While a data driven approach is finding its way into all facets of business, companies are fiercely fighting for the best data analytic skills that are available in the market, and salaries for data science roles are going in overdrive. Compared with other commonly popular IT related job positions, such as software engineers, most of big companies increased focus on acquiring data science talent goes hand in hand with the creation of a whole new set of data science roles and titles. On the other hand, Biomedical Engineers are an afterthought, and require creative means to break out into these lucrative fields. Enter: yours truely.

  I'll be graduating this year with a degree in Biomedical Engineering,so I am interested in the job placement for biomedical engineers. As we discussed above, the data scientists and analysts are really popular in the job market. Let's check differences between jobs for biomedical majors and software engineers in terms of location, salary, skill sets, experience, and degree preference. So we want to find a online employment search website to gather the in-time information and data to figure out this problem.

   CyberCoders is an innovative employment search website based in California. Cybercoders website is really clear and formatted. Since their posts have no outside links like other employment search websites, its easier to get the content of each post to construct a data frame. Also, this website focuses more on data engineering related job markets, so it is perfect for us to analyze content. Additionally, this website is well organized and frequently update since most of the jobs are posted within 10 days.

  **The web crawler is here.**

  In our project, we get the information of 5 Biomedical and 121 Software Engineer job postings on CyberCoders through web scraping, which includes the job title, id, description, post data, salary range, preferred skills, city, and state. We compare the salary of BME and SDE jobs, also including a comparison among different parts of US. Furthermore, we find the requested years of experience through regular expression, and the most important skills through Natural Language Processing techniques. We are also interested in the degree required for the job and the posting dates.
